About Al-Huda Public School

Our History

Al-Huda Public School was established in 2001 with the vision of providing quality education to the students of our community. From humble beginnings, our school has grown to become a premier institution, offering classes from 1st to 8th grade.

Over the years, we have remained committed to our core values of academic excellence, character building, and holistic development of our students.

Our Approach

Academics

We believe in providing a strong academic foundation to our students. Our experienced and dedicated faculty members ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education, covering all core subjects and languages.

Physical Activities

We understand the importance of physical well-being and encourage our students to participate in various sports and extracurricular activities. Our school has excellent facilities for outdoor and indoor games, ensuring a healthy and active lifestyle for our students.

Islamic Education

In addition to academics, we place a strong emphasis on Islamic education. Our students are taught the principles and values of Islam, instilling in them a deep sense of morality and spirituality.

Our Mission

At Al-Huda Public School, our mission is to nurture the minds and souls of our students, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible and successful individuals in society.
